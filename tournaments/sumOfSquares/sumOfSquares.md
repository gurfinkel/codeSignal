
Find the sum of squares of all integers from  `1`up to and including given  `N`.

Example

For  `n = 5`, the output should be  
`sumOfSquares(n) = 55`.

Input/Output

-   **[execution time limit] 4 seconds (js)**
    
-   **[input] integer n**
    
    _Guaranteed constraints:_  
    `1 ≤ n ≤ 10`.
    
-   **[output] integer**
